# 🛒 Retail Sales Performance Analysis

> Analyzed 9,994 retail transactions to uncover $2.3M revenue trends, regional performance gaps, and discount impact on profitability.

## 📊 Key Results
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Revenue | $2,297,200 |
| Total Profit | $286,397 |
| Total Orders | 5,009 |
| Profit Margin | 12.47% |

## 💡 Key Findings
- **West region** leads with $725K revenue & 14.94% margin
- **Discounts above 20%** turn profit negative
- **Technology** is most profitable category (17.4% margin)
- **Furniture Tables** losing -$17,725 despite high sales volume
- **Consumer segment** dominates with 50.56% of total revenue
